A Cadillac of a Cup

The following recipe is for starting off your morning the right way -- With a Cadillac

Ingredients

  • 2 - 4 scoops of yellow bag
  • 2 - 4 scoops of cocoa powder
  • 1 - 2 sweetener packs
  • 1 Fireball candy
  • 1 Peppermint candy

Instructions

  1. Add the scoops of yellow bag and cocoa to your coffee cup
  2. Add the sweetener packs and candy pieces to your coffee cup
  3. Bring water to a boil
  4. Pour water into your coffee cup with your ingredients
  5. Stir and begin your day with a Cadillac
